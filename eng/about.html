About Us

Our Mission

Build games that respect limitations and celebrate the superpowers of neurodiverse minds, creating creative, emotional, and unexpected experiences.

Our History

In March 2024, Andrews left his job as Lead Dev at a Filipino/Chinese studio to found CHUVA Games. The studio was born with the purpose of being a conscious creative space, bringing together neurodiverse talents in an environment of respect and collaboration.
