Story

Meet Gatonho, a brave little kitten born without arms who dreams of becoming a great soccer player. His world is turned upside down when the evil Slime Jackson (an imitator of Prince Jackson) steals the precious "Ring Cup" from the Street Soccer Championship!

Now Gatonho must embark on an epic adventure through a vibrant world full of challenges, collecting magical keys and rings to unlock the path to the final confrontation. Will our armless hero be able to recover the trophy and prove that courage has no limits?

This game is made to run on the Atari 2600 video game console. Here it works thanks to the Javatari.js emulator.

Trophy System

Gold Trophy (1st Place)

Collect all 4 keys AND all 4 rings using the keys in the corresponding barracks. Defeat Slime Jackson.

Silver Trophy (2nd Place)

Collect all 4 keys (blue, red, white and green) and defeat Slime Jackson.

Bronze Trophy (3rd Place)

Defeat Slime Jackson and recover the Street Soccer Cup.

Frequently Asked Questions

My kick sometimes doesn't work. Is it a bug?

Gatonho's kick has a cooldown. After kicking, you must take a step back and wait. One kick forward, one step back. Rhythm!

What is that colored square in the middle at the beginning?

It's Gatonho's dream square ball! Gives +1 damage and ranged attacks. There's a 50% chance to find it in the initial chest.

A dog keeps killing me. What should I do?

Rhythm! The Catiorão advances in a fixed pattern. Like dancing samba - one step forward, one back. You need to sway!

I died to a winged monster that looks like a bee. What was it?

That's Ozzy, the Bat. He flies in a zigzag pattern. You can beat him by luring him into position, kicking and waiting for him to return. Always with rhythm!

How to defeat the Prince Jackson Imitator Slime?

It's easier with help! Defeat Catiorões to earn powerful colored cleats (up to 2, appear as power bar). Each cleat gives +1 damage. With the dream ball, you get +1 extra and ranged attacks!

What are the keys for?

The keys open the barracks where the rings are hidden. Each key corresponds to a specific color: blue, red, white, and green. You need to collect all keys to access all rings!

What are the rings for?

The rings are part of the "Ring Cup" that was stolen by Slime Jackson. Collecting all 4 rings is essential to complete the quest and achieve the Gold Trophy!

Does the game have an ending or is it just for scoring?

The game has an ending. The score can help you, but never forget that everything is a game and the game will end one day. So play until the end and never give up. Although tomorrow will be another day!

What is this big-headed yellow character that looks like E.T.? I have bad feelings!

This is Gatonho, and don't worry, he's not E.T., he's a cat without little arms, but he never falls into holes! But if more people read the manual, E.T.s and Gatonhos would scare less, I guarantee! But we would never demand that.
