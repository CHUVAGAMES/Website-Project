Member Profile

Gabriel Flexa
Music Producer
Gabriel Flexa is a music producer at CHUVA Games studio, responsible for creating emotionally driven soundtracks with rich textures in sonic narratives that range from pop/rock/hip-hop aesthetics to traditional Amazonian regional music. Originally from Belém do Pará, he began producing his own tracks during his teenage years and has since been shaping his musical style through studio work and collaborations with artists from the Brazilian independent music scene.
