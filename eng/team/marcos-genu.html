Member Profile

Marcos Genú
Art Director
Marcos Genú has worked since 2012 with concept art, 2D animation, and asset creation for games. He has contributed to several projects, including Tap Tap Beer and games developed for major companies such as VALE and the Government of Pará. His work also includes internationally awarded games like Ghostein, recognized at Game Jam+, and a project that secured sixth place at the global BGJam. "Hi, I'm Marcos."
